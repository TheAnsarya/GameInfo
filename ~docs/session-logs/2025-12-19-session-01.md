### Changes Made

1. **Removed `games.darkrepos.com/` folder** - Flattened structure directly into `Wiki/`
2. **Platform-based organization** - Games now under `NES/`, `SNES/`, `GBA/` folders
3. **Removed redundant `_(Platform)` suffix** - `Dragon_Warrior_IV_(NES)/` → `NES/Dragon_Warrior_IV/`
4. **Consolidated infrastructure** - Templates and categories moved to `_meta/`
5. **Cleaned up orphan files** - Removed duplicate Dragon Quest I & II files
