| Item names | $28010 | 10 | Text encoded |

| Spell learning | $3c010 | 15 | Level:spell pairs |
| Battle AI | $4c010 | 19 | Tactics tables |

- **ROM addresses**: Every data table includes source ROM location
- **Descriptive text**: Added explanations for what data means
- **Structure documentation**: Data layouts with offset, size, purpose
