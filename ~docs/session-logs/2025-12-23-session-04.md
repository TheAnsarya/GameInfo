### Text Encoding Summary
- $00-$09: Numbers 0-9
- $0A-$23: Uppercase A-Z
- $24-$3D: Lowercase a-z
- $3E: Space
- $3F-$47: Punctuation (.,:!?'"-)
- $F0-$F8: Control codes (pause, color, names, etc.)
- $FE: New line
- $FF: End of string
